Hardware

Bathroom scale Same scale, same spot, same time each morning. Daily weigh-ins feed the chart in this app.
Small kitchen scale For weighing protein portions. "1.5 g/kg of bodyweight" needs grams, not guesses.
Measuring cups & spoons For electrolyte doses and water targets.

Electrolytes & supplies

Plain salt (sodium) 3–5 g of sodium per day. Plain table or sea salt is fine.
Potassium chloride ("lite salt") For 1–3 g potassium per day. Buy ahead — kidneys flush it hard on low-carb.
Magnesium glycinate or citrate 300–400 mg, with dinner. Avoid oxide — it absorbs poorly.
Sweetener — stevia drops preferred Pick something you tolerate on an empty stomach. Pure stevia drops are the safest. Stevia + erythritol blends often work but are more dubious — some people get GI issues on an empty stomach. Avoid xylitol-style polyols when fasting — intolerance gets worse without food to buffer it.
Chia pack (≈100 g) to carry Pocket emergency food. Adds a small protein/fibre buffer if you are away from your kitchen and need to hold off a bad decision.
Lean protein for week 1 Chicken breast, white fish, eggs, low-fat dairy, plus leafy greens. Remove the snacks that will tempt you on day 4.

Apps & tools on phone

Fast-access AI assistant on phone For scanning food labels and asking "how much protein in this cut?" in the store. Friction here causes decision fatigue, and decision fatigue is how the plan fails. Make the AI one tap away.
Weight-tracking app Libra Weight Manager (Android) is the established choice. Or use the Metabolica tracker on this site if it works well for you.

Calibration — do these once, before day 1

Weigh your clothes Weigh one heavier outfit and one lighter outfit on the kitchen scale. You weigh daily — you will not always strip down — so you need a good guess for the "with clothes" offset. Write the numbers somewhere you will find later.
Measure the toilet delta One morning: step on the scale, use the toilet, step on again. The difference is your typical morning toilet delta. Knowing it makes the daily weigh-in less noisy.
Measure the natural daily fluctuation Weigh in the morning, then again 2–3 hours later without eating or drinking. Roughly 100 g/hour decrease is normal (sweat, breath). Once you know your fluctuation range, you stop misreading noise as a trend.

Medical

Talk to a doctor Especially if you take medication, are pregnant, are under 18, or have any history of kidney, liver, heart, or eating-disorder issues. PSMF is a real medical intervention.
Important. A protein-sparing modified fast is a real medical intervention. Talk to a doctor before starting, especially if you take medication, are pregnant, are under 18, or have any kidney, liver, heart, or eating-disorder history. This app is a tracker and a working guide, not medical advice.
